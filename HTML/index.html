Computer Club News

Capture the Flag competitions are competitions where a team tries to solve as many challenges as they can within a specific timeframe to get as many points as possible. Challenges can range from breaking out of odd shells to privilege escalation to binary reversing to steganography with many more possibilities. Andrew Kramer (@rewzilla) will be walking through a CTF solution he's solved in the past.
